Michigan Leagues of Academic Games Steering Committee

The Steering Committee is the governing body of the Michigan Leagues of Academic Games. The Steering Committee is responsible for: Training Coaches, Arranging League Tournaments, Awards Ceremonies, and Maintaining League Records
